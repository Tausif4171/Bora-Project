HOW IT WORKS

Easy 3 step process.

STEP 01
Unlock
Unlock a quality beach chair through the Bora mobile app, from a conveniently located docking station at the beach.
STEP 02
Relax
Enjoy! Without worrying about lugging the chair around with you when the sun goes down.
STEP 03
Return
Simply return the chair to the docking station on your way off the sand. Wait for the green light to ensure its locked into place.
